Roomify is a platform designed to facilitate temporary, secure communication spaces. Whether for quick collaborations or private discussions, Roomify allows users to generate isolated rooms that automatically expire, ensuring no persistent digital footprint is left behind. Developed with a focus on simplicity and security, it leverages modern web technologies to provide a seamless user experience.
