Developed a fully interactive 3D Rubik’s Cube platform with real-time visualization and precise cube state manipulation. Integrated Kociemba’s Algorithm to generate near-optimal solutions, along with a sandbox environment for executing custom algorithms and practicing CFOP methods. Built a robust move parser supporting standard cube notation and enabled seamless switching between manual solving and automated solutions with random scramble generation.
